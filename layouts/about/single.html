Analytical Focus

My analytical focus centres on distinguishing true macroeconomic shifts from engineered intraday volatility. Rather than relying on lagging indicators, my research breaks down the underlying physics of the market.

  • Market Structure Mechanics Decoding liquidity sweeps, stop runs, and genuine Changes in the State of Delivery.
  • Systematic Trading Literacy Bridging the gap between discretionary chart reading and the quantifiable rules required for automated execution models.
  • Macro & Emerging Market Trends Providing high-level context on how capital rotation and central bank policies alter intraday dynamics.
